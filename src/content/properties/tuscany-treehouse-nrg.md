## The Experience

The Tuscany Treehouse sits on a wooded hillside near Hico, West Virginia, within striking distance of New River Gorge National Park. The structure is anchored 20 feet above the forest floor on heavy 12x12 timber posts, and you reach it by crossing a 38-foot canopy bridge from the adjacent hillside. The bridge sways slightly underfoot — a gentle reminder that you've left the ground behind.

## What Makes It Special

- **38-foot canopy bridge**: The entry experience sets the tone for the entire stay
- **Elevated perspective**: At 20 feet up, you're in the mid-canopy with birds and squirrels
- **Dual decks**: Front and back decks offer different forest views and light exposures
- **Hot tub placement**: On the front deck, surrounded by nothing but trees
- **National Park proximity**: New River Gorge Bridge and major trailheads within 20 minutes

## The Space

The interior has a Tuscan-inspired warmth with cedar-wood walls and quality furnishings. A king bed occupies the main sleeping area with views through large windows into the canopy. The full kitchen is compact but well-equipped for cooking real meals. The bathroom is modern and clean with a standard shower.

### Outdoor Living

- **Front deck**: Hot tub, seating area, and the primary view corridor
- **Back deck**: Quieter, morning sun, good for coffee
- **Fire pit**: Ground level, accessible via stairs from the treehouse
- **Canopy bridge**: 38 feet of suspended walkway connecting to the hillside parking area

## Best For

- Adventure travelers using New River Gorge as a base camp
- Climbers and rafters who want comfort after a day on the rock or river
- Couples seeking a unique entry experience (that bridge)
- Spring through fall — the canopy is most dramatic with leaves

## Getting There

From Charleston, WV, take US-19 South toward Fayetteville/Hico. The property is accessed via paved roads with a short gravel drive to the hillside parking area. The canopy bridge begins at the parking level — no stairs to reach it. Cell service is adequate in the area.
