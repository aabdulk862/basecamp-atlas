## The Experience

Lost Cove sits on a private stretch of the Whitewater River above Cashiers, North Carolina, deep within the Nantahala National Forest. The property's defining feature is a private trail system that leads to six waterfalls — including Bedrock Falls and Exit Falls — accessible only to guests. You can spend an entire day exploring cascades without seeing another person.

## What Makes It Special

- **Six private waterfalls**: A trail system exclusive to the property leads to cascades ranging from intimate slides to dramatic drops
- **Finnish sauna**: A dedicated sauna building perched on the riverbank
- **River frontage**: A private stretch of the Whitewater River with swimming holes
- **Maximum seclusion**: Deep in national forest with no visible neighbors
- **Waterfall density**: More private waterfalls than any other property in the region

## The Space

The cabin is a 3-bedroom, 3.5-bathroom retreat built with quality materials and modern finishes. The primary suite has a king bed with river views. The full kitchen is equipped for extended stays. Living areas are comfortable and well-furnished without being fussy. The real luxury here is the land — the cabin is the base camp for the waterfall trail system.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, with forest and river sounds
- **Finnish sauna**: Standalone building on the riverbank — heat up, then plunge in the river
- **Fire pit**: Stone-ringed near the river
- **Private trail system**: Maintained paths to six waterfalls
- **Swimming holes**: Multiple spots along the private river stretch

## Best For

- Waterfall chasers who want exclusive access
- Sauna enthusiasts who appreciate the heat-then-cold-plunge ritual
- Families or groups who want a multi-day adventure base
- Spring and summer when water flow is highest and swimming is ideal

## Getting There

From Asheville, take US-64 South toward Cashiers. The property is accessed via paved roads with a final stretch of maintained gravel. The drive is scenic and straightforward — no white-knuckle switchbacks. Parking is beside the cabin with a flat walk to the front door.
