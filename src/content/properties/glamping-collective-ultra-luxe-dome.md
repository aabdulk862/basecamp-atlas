## The Experience

The Glamping Collective occupies a private mountaintop west of Asheville, where geodesic domes are positioned to maximize views and privacy. The Ultra Luxe Dome is their premium tier — the best views on the mountain, the most private positioning, and the shortest walk from parking. You're 20 minutes from downtown Asheville's restaurants and breweries but feel completely removed from civilization.

## What Makes It Special

- **Mountaintop positioning**: Long-range Blue Ridge views from your private deck
- **Ultra Luxe tier**: Best views, most privacy, heated bathroom floor and toilet seat
- **Asheville proximity**: 20 minutes to downtown dining and nightlife
- **Dedicated parking**: Right at your dome — no shuttle or long walk
- **Seasonal operation**: Spring through fall, when the mountain is at its best

## The Space

The dome interior is designed for couples — a king bed faces the view wall, with quality linens and a warm aesthetic. The bathroom features heated floors (a luxury you don't expect in a dome) and modern fixtures. The kitchenette handles coffee and light meals. But the dome is really a sleeping and shelter space — the deck is where you'll spend your time.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, with mountain views
- **Fire pit**: Gas fire table on the deck
- **Gas grill**: Full-size for cooking outdoors
- **Deck seating**: Multiple chairs and a table oriented toward the view
- **Mountain panorama**: Unobstructed long-range views of the Blue Ridge

## Best For

- Couples wanting a unique stay with easy Asheville access
- First-time glampers who want luxury without roughing it
- Photographers chasing sunrise and sunset over the Blue Ridge
- Spring through fall stays (seasonal operation)

## Getting There

From Asheville, head west on I-40 toward Clyde. The property is accessed via paved mountain roads with clear signage. Dedicated parking is beside your dome. The Glamping Collective provides detailed directions and check-in instructions via email before arrival.
