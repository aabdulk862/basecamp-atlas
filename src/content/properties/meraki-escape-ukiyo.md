## The Experience

Meraki Escape occupies 200 private acres in the Blue Ridge Mountains near Brevard, North Carolina — the self-proclaimed "Land of Waterfalls" with over 250 cascades in the surrounding county. The property itself has three private waterfalls, including Forestsong Falls where optional chef-crafted dinners are served on a candlelit table beside the cascade. This is not a cabin with add-ons — it's a curated experience designed around reconnection.

## What Makes It Special

- **Three private waterfalls**: On-property cascades accessible only to guests
- **Waterfall dining**: Optional multi-course chef dinners served beside Forestsong Falls
- **In-cabin massage**: Side-by-side couples massage in your cabin or by the waterfall
- **200 acres of seclusion**: Adults-only, no neighbors, no shared spaces
- **Elevation**: At 2,600 feet, the air is noticeably cleaner and cooler

## The Space

The Ukiyo cabin is designed for intimacy. A king bed faces the forest through large windows. The walk-in shower features rainfall and body jet systems built into natural stone. The fireplace anchors the living area. The full kitchen is stocked with quality cookware and a professional blender. Covered and uncovered porches extend the living space into the forest.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Under the covered porch, surrounded by forest sounds and distant waterfall
- **Fire table**: Gas, on the uncovered porch for stargazing
- **Private trails**: Paths to three on-property waterfalls
- **Forest immersion**: 200 acres of hardwood forest at your doorstep

## Best For

- Couples seeking a curated romantic experience beyond just a cabin
- Honeymoons, anniversaries, and proposals
- Those who want optional luxury services (chef, massage) without leaving the property
- Year-round stays — the elevation keeps summers cool and winters dramatic

## Getting There

From Asheville, head south toward Brevard on US-276. The property is accessed via paved roads with a final stretch of maintained gravel. Meraki provides detailed directions and gate information before arrival. The cabin is a short flat walk from parking.
