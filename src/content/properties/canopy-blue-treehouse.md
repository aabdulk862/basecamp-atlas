## The Experience

Canopy Blue sits within the Aska Adventure Area outside Blue Ridge, Georgia — a network of trails, waterfalls, and forest roads that draws hikers and mountain bikers year-round. The treehouse is elevated among mature hardwoods with 1,200 square feet of wrap-around decking that nearly doubles the living space. From the hot tub, the swing bed, or any of the deck's seating areas, you're looking out across layers of Blue Ridge ridgelines.

## What Makes It Special

- **Deck-to-interior ratio**: 1,200 sq ft of outdoor deck vs 650 sq ft interior — the outdoors is the point
- **Swing bed**: Suspended on the deck for afternoon naps with mountain views
- **Canopy-level hot tub**: Positioned at the deck's best vantage point
- **Aska Adventure Area**: Trail network begins minutes from the property
- **Downtown Blue Ridge**: 10-minute drive to restaurants, shops, and the scenic railway

## The Space

The interior features lofted ceilings with exposed beams, a custom dining booth, and beds for six guests across a queen and full bed plus twin bunks in the loft. The full kitchen is well-stocked for cooking. A smart TV with Netflix handles rainy evenings, though the deck and fire pit tend to win.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: On the wrap-around deck with mountain views
- **Swing bed**: Suspended from the deck structure
- **Fire pit**: Ground level with dedicated seating
- **Gas grill**: Full-size Weber on the deck
- **Multiple deck zones**: Morning coffee spot, afternoon reading nook, evening hot tub

## Best For

- Groups or couples who live outdoors and want maximum deck space
- Hikers and trail runners using Aska Adventure Area
- Fall color seekers — the North Georgia mountains peak in late October
- Year-round stays with easy access to Blue Ridge dining

## Getting There

From Atlanta, take GA-400 North to Blue Ridge, then follow signs toward the Aska Adventure Area. The final approach is a paved mountain road with moderate curves. Dedicated parking is steps from the treehouse entrance — no long walks or steep stairs.
