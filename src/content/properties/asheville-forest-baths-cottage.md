## The Experience

Asheville Forest Baths is a micro-retreat concept: three luxury cottages and a treehouse sauna pavilion set within 1.5 acres of pristine mountain laurel forest in Fairview, just 15 minutes from downtown Asheville. Every guest receives a complimentary private sauna session in the elevated treehouse sauna — a ritual that sets the tone for the entire stay. The concept blends Japanese forest bathing philosophy with Appalachian forest immersion.

## What Makes It Special

- **Treehouse sauna pavilion**: An elevated sauna structure in the canopy — complimentary private session included
- **Cedar soaking tub**: Private, on your cottage deck beneath the trees
- **Forest bathing philosophy**: The entire property is designed around slowing down and sensory immersion
- **Artisan details**: Hand-painted porcelain sink, custom furnishings, curated toiletries
- **Asheville proximity**: 15 minutes to downtown restaurants and breweries

## The Space

The Cottage is intimate — designed for two. A lofted king bed sits above the main living area, creating a cozy nest with forest views through the windows. The bathroom features the hand-painted porcelain sink and quality fixtures. Custom furnishings throughout give the space a collected, intentional feel rather than a rental aesthetic. The deck with your private cedar soaking tub is the centerpiece.

### Outdoor Living

- **Cedar soaking tub**: Private, on the deck beneath mountain laurel canopy
- **Treehouse sauna**: Elevated pavilion with complimentary private session
- **Deck**: Intimate outdoor living space surrounded by rhododendron
- **Forest paths**: Wander the 1.5-acre mountain laurel grove

## Best For

- Wellness-focused travelers who appreciate ritual and intention
- Couples wanting spa-level experiences in a private setting
- Those who want Asheville access without staying in town
- Year-round stays — the sauna-to-cold-air contrast is best in winter

## Getting There

From Asheville, head east on I-40 toward Fairview. The property is accessed via paved roads in a quiet residential area. Parking is beside your cottage. The entire property is flat and easily walkable.
