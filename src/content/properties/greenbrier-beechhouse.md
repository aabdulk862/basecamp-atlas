## The Experience

The Greenbrier Beechhouse occupies 11 private acres of mature hardwood forest in Greenbrier, Tennessee — just 25 minutes north of downtown Nashville. The property consists of two connected treehouses: a main house and a separate guest pod, linked by a swing bridge that sways gently as you cross. Each structure has its own private deck and outdoor shower, creating the feeling of a private compound in the trees.

## What Makes It Special

- **Dual treehouse design**: Main house + guest pod connected by swing bridge
- **11 private acres**: Complete seclusion despite Nashville proximity
- **Outdoor showers**: Both structures have private hot-water outdoor showers
- **Swing bridge connection**: The crossing between structures is an experience in itself
- **Nashville access**: Downtown dining, music, and culture 25 minutes away

## The Space

The main treehouse is a 1-bedroom, 1-bathroom space with a king bed, open-air soaking tub, and quality finishes throughout. The guest pod adds a second bedroom and bathroom with its own private deck and outdoor shower. Together they sleep 4 comfortably with a queen sofa bed in the main house. The design is modern and warm — cedar walls, natural light, and intentional simplicity.

### Outdoor Living

- **Open-air soaking tub**: In the main treehouse, surrounded by forest
- **Outdoor showers**: One on each structure's private deck
- **Swing bridge**: Connecting the two treehouses through the canopy
- **Fire pit**: Ground level with seating
- **EV charger**: Available for electric vehicle owners
- **Private decks**: Each structure has its own outdoor living space

## Best For

- Couples wanting a nature escape with easy Nashville access
- Small groups who appreciate having separate sleeping structures
- Music lovers splitting time between the woods and Broadway
- Year-round stays — the forest setting is beautiful in every season

## Getting There

From Nashville, take I-65 North to the Greenbrier exit. The property is accessed via paved roads with a short drive to dedicated parking. The main treehouse is a flat walk from the car. The swing bridge to the guest pod begins from the main deck.
