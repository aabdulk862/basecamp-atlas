## The Experience

Inn The Ravine is the sister treehouse to Canopy Blue, operated by Blue Ridge Treehouse Rentals in the Aska Adventure Area of North Georgia. While Canopy Blue sits high with panoramic views, Inn The Ravine is tucked into a ravine — surrounded by trees on all sides with a creek running below. The effect is intimate and enveloping rather than expansive.

## What Makes It Special

- **Ravine setting**: Nestled low among the trees rather than perched above them
- **Creek soundtrack**: Running water below the structure provides constant ambient sound
- **California king bed**: Larger than standard king — a detail that matters
- **Stone fireplace**: Interior warmth and atmosphere on cool evenings
- **Secluded from sibling**: Despite being on adjacent property to Canopy Blue, fully private

## The Space

The interior is larger than Canopy Blue at approximately 800 sq ft, with a California king in the primary bedroom, a queen in the second, and a twin in the loft. The full kitchen is well-equipped with modern appliances. The stone fireplace anchors the living area. A smart TV with Netflix is available, and WiFi is strong enough for streaming.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, positioned on the deck overlooking the ravine
- **Fire pit**: Ground level near the creek
- **Deck**: Multi-level with seating areas at different elevations
- **Gas grill**: Full-size on the deck
- **Creek access**: Steps lead down to the water from the lower deck

## Best For

- Those who prefer intimate, enclosed forest settings over open views
- Couples or small families wanting a creek-side retreat
- Hikers using the Aska Adventure Area trail network
- Year-round stays — the ravine setting is dramatic in every season

## Getting There

Same access as Canopy Blue — from Atlanta, take GA-400 North to Blue Ridge, then follow signs toward the Aska Adventure Area. The final approach is a paved mountain road. Dedicated parking is steps from the treehouse entrance.
