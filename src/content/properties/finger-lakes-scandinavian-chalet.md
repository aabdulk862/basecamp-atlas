## The Experience

The Finger Lakes Scandinavian Chalet sits tucked into a wooded hillside near Dansville, New York, offering a design-forward retreat that blends Nordic minimalism with the natural beauty of the western Finger Lakes. This handcrafted home was built with energy efficiency and intentional living at its core — every detail from the custom furniture to the oversized windows connecting you to the surrounding forest has been carefully considered. The property occupies a quiet, secluded setting where light pollution is minimal and the only sounds are wind through the trees and birdsong at dawn. Guests consistently describe the atmosphere as peaceful and restorative, a place where the boundary between indoors and outdoors dissolves through walls of glass framing the forest canopy.

## What Makes It Special

- **Scandinavian design philosophy**: Custom-made furniture, clean lines, and natural materials throughout create a calm, intentional space
- **Private sauna**: A dedicated sauna for post-hike recovery or evening relaxation sessions
- **Hot tub under the stars**: Soak in the hot tub surrounded by forest with zero light pollution for exceptional stargazing
- **Floor-to-ceiling windows**: Oversized glass panels bring the forest inside from every room
- **Pellet stove**: A cozy Scandinavian pellet stove for atmospheric evenings by the fire
- **Heated bathroom floors**: Spa-quality heated floors and towel warmers in all three bathrooms

## The Space

The chalet interior features two bedrooms with queen beds, three full bathrooms with heated floors, and an open-concept living area anchored by the pellet stove. The fully equipped kitchen includes high-end induction appliances, and the living space offers both a smart TV and a projection screen for movie nights. A lower-level game room provides additional entertainment with table games and flexible seating. The design throughout emphasizes warm wood tones, natural light, and Scandinavian simplicity — nothing feels cluttered or overdone.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, set among the trees with unobstructed sky views
- **Sauna**: Dedicated sauna building for heat therapy and relaxation
- **Fire pit**: Stone-ringed with a grill for outdoor cooking and evening gatherings
- **Pond-side trail**: A walking path around the property's pond through mixed hardwood forest
- **Deck**: Spacious deck with seating for morning coffee surrounded by forest

## Best For

- Couples or small groups seeking a design-conscious nature retreat
- Hikers using the property as a base for Letchworth State Park and Finger Lakes Trail
- Wine country visitors exploring nearby Finger Lakes wineries and breweries
- Stargazers and those seeking genuine quiet away from light pollution
- Winter travelers combining sauna sessions with nearby skiing at Swain Resort

## Getting There

From I-390, take exit 4 toward Dansville and follow Route 36 south. The property is accessed via paved roads through rolling farmland and wooded hills. The final approach is a smooth driveway to dedicated parking beside the chalet. Dansville village is a 10-minute drive for groceries and dining. Letchworth State Park — named the best state park in the US in 2025 — is just 15 minutes away. The drive from Rochester is about 50 minutes; from Syracuse, roughly 90 minutes.
