## The Experience

The Scandinavian Treehouse at Terralodge strips away ornamentation and lets the forest do the talking. Elevated among mature hardwoods on the Cumberland Plateau, the structure uses clean lines, natural wood, and oversized windows to create a sense of floating within the canopy. The Nordic design philosophy — simplicity, function, warmth — translates perfectly to a forest setting.

## What Makes It Special

- **Nordic minimalism**: Light wood, clean geometry, and intentional negative space
- **Canopy immersion**: Windows on all sides place you at eye level with the treetops
- **Private hot tub**: Tucked on the deck among the branches
- **Plateau location**: 10-15 degrees cooler than surrounding valleys in summer
- **Proximity to trails**: Fiery Gizzard and Foster Falls within 15 minutes

## The Space

The interior is compact but never cramped. A king bed faces the largest window wall. The kitchen is fully equipped with modern appliances and clean countertops. The living area has a smart TV and comfortable seating, though the deck tends to pull you outside. The bathroom is simple and well-appointed with quality fixtures.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, positioned for forest views
- **Fire pit**: Below the treehouse with dedicated seating
- **Deck**: Wraps the structure with multiple vantage points
- **Forest access**: Trails begin steps from the property

## Best For

- Minimalists who appreciate design restraint
- Couples seeking a quiet plateau retreat
- Trail runners and hikers using South Cumberland as a base
- Fall color season (October–November) when the hardwoods ignite

## Getting There

Same access as all Terralodge properties — I-24 East from Nashville, exit at Monteagle. Paved roads to the property gate, smooth gravel to your parking spot. The treehouse is a short flat walk from the car.
