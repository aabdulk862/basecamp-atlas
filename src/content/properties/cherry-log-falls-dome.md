## The Experience

This dome resort in Cherry Log, Georgia sits on a property that directly borders the Chattahoochee National Forest and overlooks Fall Branch Falls. The 300-mile Benton MacKaye Trail passes through the property, meaning you can step out of your dome and onto a long-distance hiking trail without driving anywhere. Each dome is 530 square feet, uniquely styled, and positioned for privacy from its neighbors.

## What Makes It Special

- **Waterfall views**: Fall Branch Falls is visible from the property — some domes have direct sightlines
- **Trail access**: The Benton MacKaye Trail is a 5-minute walk from your dome
- **National Forest border**: The Chattahoochee National Forest begins at the property edge
- **Individual styling**: Each dome has a unique interior design theme
- **Hot tub with a view**: Positioned to face the falls or forest depending on the dome

## The Space

At 530 square feet, the dome is cozy but well-designed. A king bed occupies the primary zone with quality linens. The kitchenette handles coffee, snacks, and light meals. The bathroom is compact with modern fixtures. The dome's curved walls and overhead panels create an interesting geometric interior that photographs well and feels distinct from a standard cabin.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, positioned for waterfall or forest views
- **Fire pit**: Personal fire pit with seating
- **Outdoor seating**: Chairs and table on the deck
- **Trail access**: Benton MacKaye Trail steps away
- **Waterfall proximity**: Fall Branch Falls visible from the property

## Best For

- Hikers wanting trail-adjacent lodging with comfort
- Waterfall enthusiasts who want to wake up to cascade views
- Budget-conscious travelers seeking quality glamping under $400/night
- Year-round stays — the waterfall is most dramatic after rain

## Getting There

From Atlanta, take I-575 North to GA-515 toward Blue Ridge. Cherry Log is about 15 minutes before Blue Ridge. The property is accessed via paved roads with a short gravel drive. Parking is near your dome with a flat walk to the entrance.
