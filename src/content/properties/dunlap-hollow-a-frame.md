## The Experience

Dunlap Hollow is a luxury retreat property in Rockbridge, Ohio, set on 29 acres of rugged Hocking Hills terrain complete with its own cliffs, recess caves, seasonal waterfalls, and a scenic overlook. The A-Frame is the property's showpiece — a custom-designed structure with soaring windows and 20 skylights that flood the interior with natural light and frame the surrounding forest canopy. Designed by owners Bryant and Amy Gingerich with architectural support from Meredith Higgins, every detail reflects intentional craftsmanship. The suspended wood-burning stove anchors the open living space, while the 6-person hot tub on the deck provides the post-hike recovery ritual. With three dedicated hiking trails starting at your doorstep and Hocking Hills State Park just 15 minutes away, this is a property where the landscape is both the backdrop and the main attraction.

## What Makes It Special

- **20 skylights**: The A-frame geometry combined with skylights creates a cathedral of natural light
- **Private trail system**: Three marked trails on the property — the Overlook Trail to a valley vista, the Crescent Cave Trail through a large recess cave, and the Cliffside Trail along dramatic rock formations
- **Suspended wood-burning stove**: A striking centerpiece that heats the open-plan living space
- **6-person hot tub**: Private deck setting surrounded by forest
- **Architect-designed**: Custom-built in 2021 with warm, elegant finishes throughout
- **29 acres of private wilderness**: Cliffs, caves, and seasonal waterfalls without the state park crowds

## The Space

The A-Frame sleeps 10 across three bedrooms and a third-floor sleeping loft. The main floor features a king bedroom, full-service kitchen with modern appliances, and the dramatic open living area centered on the suspended stove. Two queen bedrooms occupy the second level, while the loft holds a king sunken bed flanked by two single Murphy beds — a space defined by the converging rooflines and those 20 skylights overhead. Two full bathrooms serve the house. Outside, the deck extends the living space with the hot tub, an outdoor propane fireplace with lounging couches, a propane grill, and a dedicated fire pit area. The property is pet-free, maintaining a pristine environment for all guests.

## Best For

- Hikers who want private trails at their doorstep plus proximity to the state park
- Couples celebrating milestones who want the entire property to themselves
- Four-season visitors — the stove and hot tub make winter stays exceptional

## Getting There

From Columbus, take US-33 South toward Logan, then follow State Route 180 toward Rockbridge. Dunlap Road is a paved county road accessible by any vehicle. The property provides gate access information before arrival, and the drive from the road to the A-Frame is short and well-maintained. Rock House and Cantwell Cliffs are 8–10 minutes away, and Old Man's Cave is a 15-minute drive south on paved state highways.
