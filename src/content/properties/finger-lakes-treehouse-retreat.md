## The Experience

The Finger Lakes Treehouse Retreat sits elevated among the trees on a 48-acre private forest property in Sodus, New York. These are genuine A-frame structures raised off the ground, blending treehouse adventure with modern luxury. Each unit is designed for couples seeking seclusion without sacrificing comfort — private entrances, no shared walls, and enough distance between cabins that you feel genuinely alone in the woods. The property sits at the northern edge of the Finger Lakes region, minutes from Sodus Bay on Lake Ontario and within easy reach of the Seneca Lake wine trail, Chimney Bluffs State Park, and Bristol Mountain ski resort.

## What Makes It Special

- **Elevated A-frame design**: Raised structures with steep rooflines and floor-to-ceiling glass walls framing the forest canopy
- **Private wood-fired hot tub**: Each unit has its own hot tub — no sharing, no scheduling
- **Communal sauna**: A shared Finnish-style sauna available to all guests on the property
- **48 acres of forest**: Enough space that each treehouse feels completely isolated
- **Year-round destination**: Ski Bristol Mountain in winter, kayak Sodus Bay in summer, tour wineries in fall

## The Space

The A-frame interior maximizes vertical space with soaring ceilings and a lofted sleeping area. A king bed sits in the peak with views through the glass wall into the tree canopy. The main level includes a full kitchen with modern appliances, a spa-like bathroom, comfortable living area, and a laptop-friendly workspace. Climate control keeps things comfortable in every season. The design is clean and modern — warm wood tones, intentional lighting, and quality finishes throughout.

### Outdoor Living

- **Wood-fired hot tub**: Private to your unit, surrounded by forest
- **Fire pit**: Dedicated area with seating for evening fires
- **Outdoor furniture**: Deck seating for morning coffee among the trees
- **Grill**: Outdoor cooking setup for warm-weather meals
- **Forest trails**: Walking paths across the 48-acre property

## Best For

- Couples seeking a romantic escape with genuine privacy
- Wine country visitors wanting a unique home base for Finger Lakes touring
- Winter travelers combining Bristol Mountain skiing with hot tub evenings
- Those who appreciate architectural design in a natural setting

## Getting There

From I-90 (New York State Thruway), take exit 42 toward Geneva/Lyons, then follow Route 14 north toward Sodus. The property is on Pilgrimport Road, accessed entirely via paved roads through flat agricultural countryside. Easy sedan access with parking beside your treehouse. The drive from Rochester is about 45 minutes; from Syracuse, about an hour.
