## The Experience

The Hummingbird Yurt at Hillside Retreats offers a distinctive glamping experience in the Hocking Hills region of southeastern Ohio. This 20-foot yurt sits on a wooded hillside, providing scenic views from a private deck where you can soak in the hot tub after a day of trail exploration. The property is managed by Hocking Lodging Company, a Superhost operation with consistently high guest ratings. Unlike a traditional cabin, the yurt's circular design and skylight create an immersive connection to the surrounding forest — you fall asleep watching stars through the roof and wake to birdsong filtering through the canvas walls. The electric fireplace adds warmth on cool evenings, and the kitchenette means you can prepare simple meals without leaving your retreat. Hillside Retreats sits in the heart of Hocking Hills, placing you within a short drive of every major trail and waterfall in the region.

## What Makes It Special

- **Private hot tub on the hillside**: Soak under the trees with views of the wooded slope below
- **Boho-styled interior**: A thoughtfully designed circular space with queen bed, skylight, and cozy seating area
- **Game barn access**: A shared pole barn with pool table, shuffleboard, darts, cornhole, and lounge seating
- **Fire pit and grill**: Your own outdoor gathering space with a gas grill for cookouts
- **Year-round comfort**: Climate control, electric fireplace, and full bathroom with glass-enclosed shower

## The Space

The Hummingbird is a single-room yurt with an open floor plan that feels spacious despite its compact footprint. The queen bed anchors the space, with a kitchenette along one wall featuring a two-burner cooktop, microwave, pod coffee maker, and mini refrigerator with freezer. The full bathroom includes a glass-enclosed shower with provided toiletries. Outside, the private deck holds the hot tub, an outdoor loveseat, and a bistro dining set. The shared game barn is a short walk across the property and includes a full bathroom, high-top seating, and a mini fridge — making it a natural gathering spot on rainy afternoons.

## Getting There

From Columbus, take US-33 South toward Logan. The property is located off Stage Road in the Logan area, accessible via fully paved state and county roads. The driveway and parking area are gravel but well-maintained and flat — no steep grades or unpaved mountain roads to navigate. A standard sedan handles the route without issue. The major Hocking Hills trailheads are all within 12 to 20 minutes by car, and the town of Logan offers grocery stores, restaurants, and gas stations within 10 minutes.
