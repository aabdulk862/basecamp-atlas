## The Experience

Sanctuary Treehouse Resort in Sevierville, Tennessee is the world's largest interactive treehouse resort — 18+ purpose-built treehouses spread across 40 private acres in the Smoky Mountain foothills. Honey Hole is their first Luxe-tier unit, designed specifically for romantic getaways. It combines genuine luxury finishes with playful treehouse elements that remind you this isn't just another cabin.

## What Makes It Special

- **Playful luxury**: Rope climbs, escape hatches, and secret ladders coexist with premium finishes
- **Wood-burning fireplace**: On the spacious outdoor deck, not just inside
- **Swing bed**: Suspended on the deck for lazy afternoons
- **Interactive resort**: 1-mile scavenger hunt trail included with every stay
- **Smoky Mountain access**: Major GSMNP trailheads within 30 minutes

## The Space

Honey Hole is a Luxe-tier treehouse with a king bed, full kitchen, and modern bathroom. The interior blends rustic wood with contemporary comfort — quality linens, proper lighting, and enough space to feel relaxed without being cavernous. The outdoor deck is expansive with the fireplace, hot tub, swing bed, and dining area all having dedicated zones.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, on the spacious deck
- **Wood-burning fireplace**: Outdoor, on the deck — evening focal point
- **Swing bed**: Suspended for gentle motion and mountain air
- **Weber grill**: Gas, full-size
- **Rope climb and escape hatch**: Playful elements connecting deck levels

## Best For

- Couples wanting romance with a sense of play and adventure
- Honeymoons and anniversaries in the Smokies
- Those who want treehouse magic without sacrificing modern comfort
- Year-round stays — the outdoor fireplace makes winter evenings special

## Getting There

From Sevierville, the resort is accessed via paved roads with private parking beside each treehouse. No shared lots or shuttle rides. Dollywood is 10 minutes away, Gatlinburg 15 minutes, and the national park entrance 20 minutes. The resort provides gate codes and detailed directions before arrival.
