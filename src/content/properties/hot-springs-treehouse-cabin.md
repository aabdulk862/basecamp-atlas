## The Experience

Hot Springs is one of those rare Appalachian Trail towns where the trail literally crosses Main Street. The Treehouse Cabins sit on 13 acres bordering Pisgah National Forest on what the owners call "The Magic Mountain" — just 5 minutes above the historic village. Cabin 4 is the most private of the four units, with a king bed, private hot tub, and views into the national forest. The French Broad River and natural mineral hot springs are minutes away.

## What Makes It Special

- **Appalachian Trail junction**: The AT crosses through Hot Springs — thru-hikers resupply here
- **French Broad River**: Kayaking, tubing, and swimming minutes from the cabin
- **Natural mineral springs**: Hot Springs is named for its geothermal mineral soaking pools
- **Pisgah National Forest border**: 13 acres of private land meeting public wilderness
- **Swimming hole**: On-property creek access for warm-weather dips

## The Space

Cabin 4 is a 4-season structure with a king bed, full kitchen, and modern bathroom. The fireplace provides warmth and atmosphere on cool evenings. The interior is clean and well-maintained — not luxury-tier but comfortable and honest. The real draw is the setting: forest on all sides, mountain views from the porch, and the sound of Spring Creek below.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, on the cabin's deck
- **Fireplace**: Interior, wood-burning
- **Gas grill**: On the porch for cooking outdoors
- **Swimming hole**: Creek access on the property
- **Communal fire pit**: Shared gathering space (optional)

## Best For

- Hikers wanting AT access with comfort at the end of the day
- River lovers — the French Broad is ideal for kayaking and tubing
- Budget-conscious travelers seeking quality under $300/night
- Those who want to soak in natural mineral springs after a hike

## Getting There

From Asheville, take US-25/70 North through Marshall toward Hot Springs. The cabins are 5 minutes above the village on a paved road. The final stretch is a gentle grade with no tight switchbacks. Parking is beside your cabin.
