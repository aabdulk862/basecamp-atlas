## The Experience

Bolt Farm is an award-winning adults-only retreat perched on a mountaintop in the Upstate South Carolina foothills. Designed by Seth Bolt of NEEDTOBREATHE and built by his father Larry — a licensed custom home builder with 40+ years of experience — every structure on the property reflects both artistic vision and construction expertise. The Luxe Dome is their largest unit: 1,175 square feet of private space including the dome interior, wraparound deck, and fire pit area.

## What Makes It Special

- **Maximum privacy**: Adults-only, each unit completely secluded from others
- **Mountaintop positioning**: Long-range views across the Blue Ridge foothills
- **Curated experiences**: Optional romance packages, candlelit turndowns, in-room bath rituals
- **Condé Nast recognition**: Made the ballot for Traveler's Readers' Choice Awards
- **Architectural intention**: Every detail designed to foster intimacy and reconnection

## The Space

The geodesic dome's geometry creates a dramatic interior volume. A king bed is positioned to face the best view angle. The kitchenette handles light meals and morning coffee. The bathroom is well-appointed with quality fixtures. But the real living happens on the deck — the hot tub, fire pit, and seating areas all face the mountain panorama.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, with mountain views
- **Fire pit**: Dedicated area with comfortable seating
- **Wraparound deck**: Multiple zones for different times of day
- **Mountain views**: Unobstructed long-range vistas

## Best For

- Couples celebrating milestones — anniversaries, honeymoons, proposals
- Design-conscious travelers who appreciate intentional spaces
- Those seeking genuine disconnection with luxury comfort
- Year-round stays — the mountaintop setting is compelling in every season

## Getting There

From Greenville, SC, take SC-11 (Cherokee Foothills Scenic Highway) toward Walhalla. The property is accessed via paved mountain roads. Bolt Farm provides detailed directions and gate access information before arrival. The drive from the gate to your dome is short and paved.
