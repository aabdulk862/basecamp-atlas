## The Experience

Skyline Yurt sits in the mountains near Front Royal, Virginia — just an hour from Washington, DC but feeling like another world entirely. The structure is a modern interpretation of the traditional yurt: timber-framed, insulated for four seasons, and crowned with a massive glass dome skylight that fills the interior with natural light and frames the night sky.

## What Makes It Special

- **Glass dome skylight**: The circular ceiling window is the centerpiece — stars at night, dappled light by day
- **Round architecture**: The yurt's geometry creates a warm, enveloping space unlike any rectangular cabin
- **Elevated hot tub deck**: Private, with mountain views stretching across the valley
- **Activity density**: Archery, pool table, PS5, fire pit — rainy days are covered
- **Shenandoah proximity**: Skyline Drive entrance is 15 minutes away

## The Space

The yurt sleeps 8 across three bedrooms and two bathrooms, but works beautifully for couples who want space to spread out. The main living area has vaulted ceilings rising to the glass dome, a wood-burning stove, and a full gourmet kitchen with island seating. A loft above holds the pool table. The 75-inch smart TV has streaming services, but the stove and skylight tend to win the evening.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: 7-person, on an elevated deck with mountain views
- **Fire pit**: Stone-ringed with premium seating
- **Archery range**: Full setup with equipment provided
- **Cornhole and lawn games**: On the flat clearing beside the yurt

## Best For

- Couples or small groups wanting a unique architectural stay
- DC/Richmond residents seeking a quick mountain escape
- Old Rag hikers who want luxury after the scramble
- Four-season stays — the wood stove makes winter magical

## Getting There

From Richmond, take I-66 West or US-211 toward Front Royal. The yurt is accessed via paved county roads with a short gravel drive to dedicated parking. No steep grades. The property provides detailed directions and a gate code 24 hours before check-in.
