## The Experience

Canopy Ridge sits on a private 40-acre parcel bordering Great Smoky Mountains National Park. The treehouse is built into a cluster of mature oaks at the property's highest point — you cross a 30-foot suspended bridge from the parking area to reach the front door. Below you, nothing but forest floor and ferns. Above, the canopy filters light into shifting green patterns all day long.

## What Makes It Special

- **True treehouse construction**: Load-bearing bolts into living trees, not a cabin on stilts
- **Suspended bridge entry**: The 30-foot walkway sways gently and sets the tone immediately
- **Canopy-level hot tub**: Positioned on a reinforced platform among the branches
- **Maximum privacy**: The nearest neighbor is over a mile away through dense forest
- **National Park proximity**: Five major trailheads within 30 minutes

## The Space

The lower level is an open living area with a wood-burning fireplace, leather armchair, and floor-to-ceiling windows on three sides. A spiral staircase leads to the upper sleeping loft with a king bed positioned directly beneath a skylight. The bathroom has a glass-walled rain shower with views into the canopy.

### Amenities Breakdown

- Full kitchen with gas range, dishwasher, and local coffee beans
- Hammock deck suspended between two oaks (separate from the hot tub deck)
- Ground-level fire pit with Adirondack chairs (accessible via a second staircase)
- Bluetooth speaker system throughout
- Starlink internet for remote workers

## Best For

- Nature immersion without sacrificing comfort
- Couples or solo travelers who want genuine seclusion
- Hikers using the Smokies as a base camp
- Spring wildflower season (April–May) and fall color (mid-October)

## Getting There

From Charlotte, take I-40 West through Asheville and continue into the Smokies corridor. The final 3 miles are a paved county road with a gentle grade. The property gate has a keypad — code provided 24 hours before check-in. Cell service is limited on-site but the Starlink connection is reliable.
