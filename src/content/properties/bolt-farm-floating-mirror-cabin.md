## The Experience

The Floating Mirror Cabin is Bolt Farm's most architecturally daring unit. Clad in one-way mirrored panels, the structure reflects its surroundings so completely that it appears to hover — a shimmering ghost among the trees. From inside, every surface is a window into the forest. The cabin is elevated off the ground, adding to the floating illusion and providing unobstructed sightlines in every direction.

## What Makes It Special

- **Disappearing architecture**: Mirror cladding renders the cabin nearly invisible from outside
- **Floating elevation**: Raised off the forest floor, enhancing the weightless illusion
- **Total privacy**: The most secluded unit on the Bolt Farm property
- **Arhaus collaboration**: Interior furnished with curated Arhaus pieces
- **Sensory design**: The interplay of reflection, transparency, and forest creates a meditative state

## The Space

The interior is intimate and intentional. A king bed faces the largest glass wall. The kitchenette is minimal but functional — this is a stay about presence, not cooking elaborate meals. Quality linens, curated toiletries, and thoughtful details throughout. The bathroom is compact with premium fixtures.

### Outdoor Living

- **Hot tub**: Private, positioned among the trees
- **Fire pit**: Dedicated seating area for evening warmth
- **Deck**: Wraps the cabin with views into the canopy
- **Mountain panorama**: Long-range views from the elevated position

## Best For

- Architecture and design enthusiasts seeking a one-of-a-kind stay
- Couples wanting maximum privacy and romance
- Photographers drawn to the mirror-meets-forest visual
- Those who loved Villa Luna and want a similar but distinct experience

## Getting There

Same access as all Bolt Farm properties — SC-11 from Greenville toward Walhalla. Paved roads to the property gate, then a short drive to dedicated parking near your cabin. Flat walk from car to cabin.
