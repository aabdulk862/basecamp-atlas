## The Case for Winter Retreats

Winter strips the mountains to their bones. Without leaf cover, ridgeline views stretch further, stargazing improves dramatically, and the contrast of hot water against cold air makes every soak feel earned. These properties are designed for exactly this.

### What Makes a Great Winter Stay

- **Hot tub as centerpiece:** Every featured property has a private hot tub accessible in any weather
- **Heated interiors:** Radiant floors, fireplaces, or efficient heating systems keep the cabin warm without drafts
- **Sedan-friendly access:** No chains or 4WD required — all roads are maintained through winter
- **Shorter drive times:** Off-peak means less traffic on mountain roads

### Winter Planning Notes

January and February offer the best rates and availability. Light snow is possible above 3,500 feet but rarely accumulates enough to close roads. Pack layers for the walk between cabin and hot tub — the temperature difference is part of the experience.
